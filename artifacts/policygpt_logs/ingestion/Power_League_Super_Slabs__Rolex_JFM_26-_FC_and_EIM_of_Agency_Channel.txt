=== Document Summary ===
Purpose:  
The Power League Super Slabs JFM'26 contest incentivizes Field Counsellors (FCs) and Elite Insurance Managers (EIMs, incl. 3Z) within HDFC Life’s Tied Agency channel (including General Agency) during Jan–Mar 2026.

Scope & Applicability:  
- Applies to all FCs and EIMs in the Tied Agency, covering business conducted in the specified period.
- FCs must achieve ≥85% 13th Month Persistency for relevant policies (issued Jan 2024–Dec 2024; renewed Jan 2025–Apr 2026).
- Exception: Persistency rule does not apply to EIMs, FCs without 2024–25 business, and FCs coded on/after 1 Jan 2025.

Key Rules/Processes:  
- Strict enforcement of persistency: non-compliance results in total forfeit of contest benefits, regardless of reason or client action.

Key Entities:  
- HDFC Life DPPM, Field Counsellors, Elite Insurance Managers (incl. 3Z), Tied Agency, General Agency.

Major Topics:  
- Contest eligibility, persistency requirements, exceptions, benefits revocation criteria.

The Power League Super Slabs JFM'26 contest, run by HDFC Life's DPPM, incentivizes Field Counsellors (FCs) and Elite Insurance Managers (EIMs) in the Tied Agency channel for high First Year First Premium (FYFP) production during January–March 2026. Raw text:
The contest is named Power League Super Slabs JFM'26 and is issued by HDFC Life's DPPM (Distribution Programs & Performance Management) department. The contest is designed to incentivise high FYFP (First Year First Premium) production among Field Counsellors and Elite Insurance Managers during the January–March 2026 period. The contest is applicable to all FCs (Field Counsellors) and EIMs (Elite Insurance Managers) of the Tied Agency channel , including EIMs designated as 3Z and FCs who are tagged under General Agency (business conducted under their self code). All rewards earned under Power League Super Slabs JFM'26 are "Over and Above" any existing FC Monthly/Annual Programs, M0–M3 Program incentives, and the ACE Program. For EIMs specifically, rewards are over and above EIM Monthly/Annual Programs and the M0–M3 Program Incentive scheme, with the exception of Product Fee and Renewal Product Fee components. The ACE program's over-and-above benefit does not apply to EIMs. The primary contest window covers a login period from 1st January 2026 to 31st March 2026 with conversion permitted until the end of the financial year (31st March 2026 financial year-end). This window applies to all cash/voucher slabs (₹1 Cr. and above) as well as the newly added 70 Lacs Rolex slab. The 52 Lacs FYFP slab had a login period running from 1st January 2026 to 31st January 2026 , with a conversion deadline of 10th February 2026 . This slab is now marked CLOSED as both the login window and conversion cut-off have passed. The 60 Lacs FYFP slab had a login period from 1st January 2026 to 28th February 2026 , with a conversion deadline of 10th March 2026 . This slab is marked CLOSED as both windows have elapsed.

This section explains key payout calculation terms for Power League Super Slabs:  
- FYC (First Year Collection) is the sum of premiums received from the 2nd to 12th policy month.

Raw text:
FYC is essentially the collection of premiums between the 2nd and 12th month of any policy as and when received. For example, in a policy with monthly mode of ₹10,000, the FYC would be ₹10,000 for each month where premium is received from the 2nd through the 12th month of the policy.
